Bob's Computer Repair Shop

Bob's Computer Repair Shop began in Grandpa Bob's garage in 1991. Since then, our family-owned business has grown from its humble beginnings into a fully fledged business that employees a wide variety of computer experts ready to solve any and all of your computer problems.

Our newly remodeled store opened in 2018. With both in-person and online customer services, Bob's Computer Repair Store is always ready to handle your computer needs. We offer the best services and prices to all types of customers, ranging from local college students to corporations based in downtown Omaha. In the spirit of our late grandfather, we proudly offer our services with a 100% guaranteed satisfcation or your money back.

From password resets to a fried motherboard, software to hardware, iOS or Microsoft, we’ve seen it all. Our vast database maintains a full registry of all computer models and parts. We even offer assistance for custom-made computers, tablets, smartphones, and all accessories. On average, consumers lose 30% of the value of their devices in just a year. With our repair services, however, you can help extend your tech’s life by utilizing proper service care from our professionals.

To date we have offered over 100,000 repair services, earning the Annual Bellevue Technical Repair Award from the city of Bellevue 10 years in a row. We are active in our community and prefer a personal relationship with our customers.

As grandfather Bob always said, "There's nothing we can't fix."
